Getting Started

Your first steps with Helensvale Connect

How to Register for Early Access

Step-by-step guide to joining our pre-launch community and securing your spot for priority access.

1

Visit our homepage and scroll to the "Get Early Access" section

2

Select your role (Customer, Business, Entrepreneur, or Delivery Partner)

3

Fill in your details and submit the form

4

Check your email for confirmation and next steps

What Happens After Registration?

Once you register for early access, you'll receive a welcome email with your unique member ID and access to exclusive updates.

  • Welcome email with member benefits
  • Regular updates on platform development
  • Priority access when we launch
  • Special early adopter pricing

For Businesses

Everything you need to succeed on our platform

Preparing Your Business for Launch

Get ready to hit the ground running when the platform goes live.

  • Gather high-quality photos of your products/services
  • Prepare detailed service descriptions
  • Set competitive pricing for your market
  • Plan your operating hours and availability
  • Set up your preferred payment methods

Business Registration Requirements

What you'll need to register your business on our platform:

  • Valid business registration (if applicable)
  • National ID or passport
  • Business address and contact details
  • Bank account or mobile money details
  • Business category and service descriptions

For Customers

Making the most of your marketplace experience

How to Find and Book Services

Discovering and booking the services you need is simple:

1

Use the search bar or browse by category

2

Filter by location, price, and ratings

3

Read reviews and check business profiles

4

Select your preferred time and book

Safety and Security Tips

Stay safe while using our platform:

  • Always verify business credentials and reviews
  • Use our secure payment system for all transactions
  • Meet in public places for pickup services
  • Report any suspicious activity immediately
  • Keep records of all your transactions

Payment Help

Understanding payments, billing, and transactions

Supported Payment Methods

We support all major payment methods used in Zimbabwe:

  • EcoCash: Most popular mobile money platform
  • OneMoney: NetOne's mobile money service
  • Bank Transfers: Direct bank-to-bank transfers
  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa and Mastercard accepted
  • Cash on Service: Pay when service is delivered

Transaction Fees and Pricing

Transparent pricing with no hidden fees:

  • Customers: No booking fees for most services
  • Businesses: Small commission on completed transactions
  • Payment processing: Standard rates apply
  • Refunds: Full refund policy for cancelled bookings
