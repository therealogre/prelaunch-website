Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

When will the platform launch?

We're currently in pre-launch phase with early access registration open. Full platform launch is planned for Q2 2025.

How can I join as a business?

Register for early access and select "Business" as your role. You'll receive priority onboarding when we launch.

What payment methods do you support?

We support EcoCash, OneMoney, bank transfers, and major credit cards - all the payment methods Zimbabweans prefer.

Is there a mobile app?

Our web platform is fully mobile-optimized. Native mobile apps for iOS and Android are in development.
