Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Is the Starter plan really free?

Yes! The first 1,000 businesses to register get the Starter plan free for their first year. After that, it's just $5/month - still very affordable for growing businesses.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept EcoCash, OneMoney, ZIPIT, bank transfers, and major credit cards. We've designed our payment system specifically for Zimbabwe's financial ecosystem.

Can I change plans anytime?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

Do you take a commission on sales?

We take a small 8% transaction fee on completed sales to cover payment processing, platform maintenance, and customer support. This is competitive with similar platforms.
