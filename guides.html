For New Users

Get started with Helensvale Connect and prepare for launch

How to Register for Early Access

Step-by-step guide to joining our pre-launch community

  • Visit the homepage and scroll to "Get Early Access"
  • Select your role (Customer, Business, Entrepreneur, or Delivery Partner)
  • Fill in your details accurately
  • Check your email for confirmation

What to Expect After Registration

Understanding your early access benefits and timeline

  • Welcome email with member ID
  • Regular development updates
  • Priority access when we launch
  • Exclusive early adopter pricing

For Businesses

Prepare your business for digital marketplace success

Business Preparation Checklist

Essential steps to get your business ready for launch

  • Gather high-quality product/service photos
  • Write compelling service descriptions
  • Research competitive pricing in your area
  • Set up your mobile money accounts (EcoCash, OneMoney)
  • Plan your operating hours and availability

Digital Marketing Basics

Simple strategies to attract customers online

  • Create a professional business profile
  • Use keywords customers search for
  • Respond quickly to customer inquiries
  • Maintain consistent service quality

For Delivery Partners

Start earning with our delivery network

Delivery Partner Requirements

What you need to become a delivery partner

  • Valid national ID or passport
  • Reliable transport (bicycle, motorbike, or car)
  • Smartphone with internet access
  • EcoCash or OneMoney account for payments
  • Basic knowledge of your delivery area

Earning Projections

Realistic income expectations based on market research

  • Part-time (10 hours/week): $30-50 per week projected
  • Full-time (40 hours/week): $120-200 per week projected
  • Peak hours (evenings/weekends) pay 20% more
  • Bonuses for consistent high ratings
