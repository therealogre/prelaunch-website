Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Helensvale Connect

General Questions

What is Helensvale Connect?

Helensvale Connect is Zimbabwe's premier digital marketplace connecting local businesses with their communities. We provide a platform where customers can discover, book, and pay for services while businesses can reach new customers and grow their operations.

When will the platform launch?

We're currently in pre-launch phase with early access registration open. Full platform launch is planned for Q2 2025. Early access members will get priority access and special pricing when we go live.

Is Helensvale Connect free to use?

For customers, browsing and booking services is free. For businesses, we offer various subscription plans starting from basic free listings to premium plans with advanced features. Check our pricing page for detailed information.

Which cities do you serve?

We're launching nationwide across Zimbabwe, covering major cities including Harare, Bulawayo, Mutare, Gweru, Kwekwe, Kadoma, Masvingo, Chinhoyi, Marondera, and many more. Rural areas are also supported where internet connectivity allows.

For Businesses

How can I register my business?

Register for early access by selecting "Business" as your role. You'll receive priority onboarding when we launch, including step-by-step guidance to set up your business profile, services, and payment methods.

What types of businesses can join?

We welcome all types of service businesses including salons, automotive services, home services, food delivery, retail, professional services, entertainment, and more. If you provide a service to customers, you can join our platform.

How do I get paid?

We support multiple payment methods including EcoCash, OneMoney, bank transfers, and direct deposits. Payments are processed securely and transferred to your preferred account within 24-48 hours of service completion.

What are the fees for businesses?

We charge a small commission on completed transactions (typically 5-10%) plus optional subscription fees for premium features. Early access members get special reduced rates. Full pricing details are available on our pricing page.

For Customers

How do I find services near me?

Use our location-based search to find services in your area. You can filter by distance, price, ratings, and availability. Our platform shows you the closest and highest-rated businesses first.

How do I book a service?

Simply browse services, select your preferred business, choose an available time slot, and confirm your booking. You'll receive instant confirmation and can track your booking status in real-time.

Can I cancel or reschedule bookings?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule bookings through your account dashboard. Cancellation policies vary by business, but most allow free cancellation up to 24 hours before the scheduled service.

How do I leave reviews?

After your service is completed, you'll receive a prompt to rate and review your experience. Your honest feedback helps other customers make informed decisions and helps businesses improve their services.

Payments & Security

What payment methods do you accept?

We support EcoCash, OneMoney, bank transfers, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and cash on service delivery. Choose the payment method that works best for you.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and security measures to protect all payment information. We never store your full payment details on our servers and comply with international security standards.

What if I have a payment dispute?

Our customer support team handles all payment disputes fairly and quickly. We have a structured dispute resolution process that protects both customers and businesses. Contact us immediately if you have any payment concerns.

Do you offer refunds?

Refund policies depend on the specific service and business. Most businesses offer full refunds for cancellations made within their cancellation window. For service quality issues, we work with both parties to find a fair resolution.

Technical Questions

Do you have a mobile app?

Our web platform is fully mobile-optimized and works perfectly on all smartphones and tablets. Native mobile apps for iOS and Android are in development and will be available soon after launch.

What if I have slow internet connection?

Our platform is optimized for Zimbabwe's internet conditions. We use data compression, offline capabilities where possible, and lightweight design to ensure smooth operation even on slower connections.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a secure reset link within minutes. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact support.

Can I use the platform offline?

Some features work offline including viewing your bookings, business information, and saved searches. However, real-time features like booking, payments, and messaging require an internet connection.
